MAX98095 audio CODEC

This device supports I2C only.

Required properties:

- compatible : "maxim,max98095".

- reg : The I2C address of the device.

Optional properties:

- clocks: The phandle of the master clock to the CODEC

- clock-names: Should be "mclk"

Example:

max98095: codec@11 {
	compatible = "maxim,max98095";
	reg = <0x11>;
};
